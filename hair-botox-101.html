The Basics

What is Hair Botox?

Hair Botox is a misnomer for any deep-conditioning hair treatment that uses fillers like keratin and collagen to repair damaged and broken hair fibers. Unlike its namesake in skincare, there are absolutely no injections or needles involved.

The treatment works by filling in any gaps or thin areas on each individual hair strand, much like how dermal fillers work on the skin. The result? Hair that looks fuller, healthier, and more vibrant from the very first application.

Professional Hair Botox treatments penetrate the hair cortex, delivering concentrated doses of vitamins, amino acids, antioxidants, and natural proteins that rebuild the hair from within. This isn't a temporary fix or a surface-level coating — it's a deep restoration that lasts for months.

Myth vs. Reality

Common Misconceptions

Let's clear up some common misunderstandings about Hair Botox treatments.

Myth #1 "It's only for damaged hair."
+

The Truth: While Hair Botox is exceptional at repairing damage, it's far more than a rescue treatment. It's also perfect for anyone wanting extra hydration, more shine, and smoother texture — even on healthy hair. Think of it as a premium maintenance treatment that takes good hair and makes it extraordinary.

Myth #2 "It's the same as skin Botox."
+

The Truth: Hair Botox has absolutely nothing to do with Botulinum toxin (Botox) used in cosmetic procedures. The name comes from the rejuvenating, restorative effects it has on hair — similar to how skin Botox "rejuvenates" the face. Hair Botox involves zero injections, zero needles, and zero toxins. It's simply a deep-conditioning treatment powered by natural proteins and vitamins.

Myth #3 "It contains harmful chemicals."
+

The Truth: Deeply Rooted Hair Botox treatments are 100% formaldehyde-free and chemical-free. Our formulas are crafted with natural proteins, vitamins, and botanicals that are safe for all hair types — including during pregnancy. We believe in treatments that heal, not harm.

Professional Guide

Steps on How to Use Hair Botox

Follow these professional steps for the best Hair Botox results — whether you're a licensed stylist or learning the process.

1

Shampoo & Prep

Shampoo the client's hair with a clarifying shampoo 1–2 times, depending on how dirty the hair is. This removes buildup and preps the hair for treatment.

2

Towel Dry

Towel dry the hair thoroughly to remove as much water as possible. Ensure the hair is damp but not dripping.

3

Prepare the Treatment

Prepare the treatment using non-metallic bowls and brushes to avoid chemical reactions.

4

Measure & Mix

Measure and mix 1–1.5 oz of Hair Botox product in a non-metallic bowl. Adjust the quantity based on the hair's length and density.

5

Section & Apply

Divide the hair into manageable sections. Apply the product ½ inch away from the scalp. For tangled hair, pre-apply the product to make sectioning easier.

6

Distribute Evenly

Distribute the product evenly through the hair using a comb. Remove any excess product to minimize fumes during the process.

7

Process & Blow-Dry

Wrap the hair with plastic and let the treatment process for 30 minutes and blow-dry the hair completely until it is smooth and dry.

8

Flat Iron

Flat iron the hair section by section, passing the iron 5–7 times on each section. Adjust the temperature to suit the hair type (300–400°F).

9

Post-Treatment Recommendation

Make sure to recommend the best sulfate-free shampoo — no salt, no chlorine — and hair spray to maximize the effectiveness of the treatment.

Product-Specific Guides

Treatment Application Guides

Detailed step-by-step instructions for each Deeply Rooted Hair Botox product.

Hair Botox "Michelle" Treatment

Application Guide
Step 1

Hair Preparation

Wash the client's hair with clarifying shampoo to remove buildup and oils. Rinse thoroughly and blow-dry until slightly damp.

Step 2

Blow Drying

Blow-dry the hair with moderate heat until 80% dry to open the cuticles for better treatment absorption.

Step 3

Sectioning & Application

Divide the hair into four sections using a non-metallic bowl and brush. Apply the product ½ inch from the scalp. Avoid over-saturation and comb through to distribute evenly.

Step 4

Processing & Drying

Let the treatment sit for 60 minutes. Do not rinse. Blow-dry completely with a non-metallic brush.

Step 5

Sealing the Treatment

Flat iron the hair again and flat iron each part 4–7 times at 380–400°F.

✦

Post-Treatment Care

No washing, hats, ties, or clips for 72 hours. Use sulfate-free shampoo every 2–3 days and avoid salt-based products to maintain results.

Hair Botox "Cynty" Treatment

Application Guide
Step 1

Hair Preparation

Wash the client's hair 1–3 times with a clarifying shampoo to remove buildup. Towel dry thoroughly until the hair is damp.

Step 2

Prepare the Product

Mix 1–1.5 oz of Cynty Hair Botox in a non-metallic bowl, adjusting the amount based on hair length and thickness.

Step 3

Sectioning & Application

Divide the hair into sections and apply the product ½ inch away from the scalp. Use a wide-tooth comb for even distribution, detangling and sectioning.

Step 4

Distribute Evenly

Comb through the hair to distribute the product evenly and remove excess to minimize fumes during the process.

Step 5

Processing & Drying

Cover the hair with a plastic wrap and let it process for 30 minutes. Blow-dry the hair completely, then flat iron each section, passing the iron 5–7 times at 300–400°F.

✦

Post-Treatment Care

No washing hair, ties, or clips for 72 hours. Use sulfate-free shampoo every 2–3 days and avoid salt-based products to maintain results.

Hair Botox "Lea" Treatment

Application Guide
Step 1

Hair Preparation

Wash the client's hair 1–3 times with clarifying shampoo. Towel dry thoroughly until damp but not dripping.

Step 2

Prepare the Product

Mix 1–1.5 oz of Lea Hair Botox in a non-metallic bowl. Adjust amount based on hair length, density, and damage level.

Step 3

Sectioning & Application

Divide hair into manageable sections. Apply the product ½ inch away from the scalp using a non-metallic brush. Comb through for even distribution.

Step 4

Processing & Drying

Cover hair with plastic wrap and let it process for 45–60 minutes. Remove excess product, then blow-dry completely until smooth.

Step 5

Sealing the Treatment

Flat iron each section 5–8 times at 380–410°F. Lea's maximum-strength formula requires thorough heat sealing for optimal results.

✦

Post-Treatment Care

No washing, hats, ties, or clips for 72 hours. Use sulfate-free shampoo every 2–3 days. Avoid salt-based products and chlorine to maintain results for up to 6 months.
