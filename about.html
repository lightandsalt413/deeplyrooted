The Beginning

From the Chair to Your Hands

Deeply Rooted Hair Essentials was born from years of passion behind the stylist's chair. Founded by Jane Magan Chang of Janerockstar Salon in Artesia, California, the brand grew out of a simple yet powerful mission: to bring professional-grade hair care to both licensed stylists and everyday clients who deserve the best.

After seeing firsthand the transformative power of Hair Botox treatments on clients of every hair type and texture, Jane set out to make these professional formulations accessible — without ever compromising on quality or integrity.

Located at 18300 Gridley Rd Suite 230, Artesia, CA, Janerockstar Salon remains the heart and home of Deeply Rooted — a place where clients experience the treatments firsthand, and where stylists come to learn, source, and grow.

Our Foundation

The Meaning Behind the Name

"They will be like a tree planted by the water that sends out its roots by the stream. It does not fear when heat comes; its leaves are always green. It has no worries in a year of drought and never fails to bear fruit."
— Jeremiah 17:8

The name "Deeply Rooted" is not just a brand identity — it's a declaration of values. Just as the tree in Jeremiah 17:8 is grounded by its roots and nourished by the stream, we believe that true beauty begins at the foundation. Healthy scalp. Salon-smooth hair.

When your hair is deeply rooted in proper care, quality ingredients, and professional treatment, it doesn't just survive — it thrives. No matter the season, no matter the stress, well-cared-for hair bears fruit. It shines. It grows. It endures.

That philosophy guides every product we formulate, every client we serve, and every stylist we partner with. We are not just selling hair care — we are cultivating confidence, one strand at a time.
